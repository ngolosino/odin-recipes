Pizza


Home


Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni sausage
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped black olives

Steps

  • In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  • In a large bowl, combine flour, salt and oil. Stir in the yeast mixture. When the dough has pulled together, turn it out onto a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth, about 8 minutes. Lightly oil a large bowl, place the dough in the bowl and turn to coat with oil. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 1 hour.
  • Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Punch down the risen dough and turn it out onto a lightly floured surface. Form dough into a round and roll out into a pizza crust shape. Transfer to a lightly greased pizza pan.
  • Spoon pizza sauce over crust and evenly distribute with a spoon. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and top with pepperoni slices, mushrooms, green pepper, onion, and olives.
  • Bake in preheated oven until cheese and crust are golden brown, about 15 to 20 minutes.
